ABOUT THE PROJECT

LODHA HINJEWADI PHASE 3 OFFERS METICULOUSLY DESIGNED HOMES IN ONE OF PUNE'S MOST SOUGHT-AFTER LOCATIONS.

NESTLED IN THE HEART OF HINJEWADI, PUNE'S PREMIER IT HUB, THIS RESIDENTIAL PROJECT COMBINES LUXURY, CONVENIENCE, AND CONNECTIVITY. WITH SEAMLESS ACCESS TO WORKPLACES, EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS, HEALTHCARE FACILITIES, AND ENTERTAINMENT OPTIONS, IT REPRESENTS THE PERFECT BLEND OF MODERN LIVING AND STRATEGIC LOCATION.

THE PROJECT FEATURES THOUGHTFULLY DESIGNED 2 AND 3 BHK APARTMENTS WITH SPACIOUS LAYOUTS, PREMIUM FINISHES, AND ABUNDANT NATURAL LIGHT.
